Late Pastor Chijioke Apostle Eze

Pastor and Founder of Alpha Covenant Church

“What will Jesus Do?” Everyone claims to be Christ-like but when situations arise you know who truly loves God. Usually i judge my every action by a simple formula: WWJD What Will Jesus Do?.
--Pastor Chijioke Eze

Pastor Eze, who prefers to describe himself just as a teacher and preacher of the word, is also the founder of Alpha Covenant Church; a local church in the village off Eleme in Rivers State founded upon sound principles and the knowledge of the word of God. Throughout his life time he believed that the word of God should be free, unaldultrated and not always convenient to the ears of the listeners.

Pastor Eze was a mentor and a role model to the pastors of his time. i can convinienetly say he impacted the calm and reeseved spirit of my life today. he was an author, a composer and a teacher

Below is a brief timeline of Pastor Eze's life and some of his achievement.

A Brief Timeline of His Adorable Life

  • 1952 - He was born Eastern Nigeria in the town of Agburuike Nsulu in what is today known as Isiala Ngwa in Abia State
  • 1960 - gets admitted into the prestigious Nbawsi Secondary School
  • 1967 - Biafra war broke out so he went to serve his uncle who was a soldier in Enugu. He recounted how his Uncle's wife wanted to sleep with him
    "she will dress up in her underwear and be constantly trying to give me instructions half naked even when i turn away she will come to face me. I remembered Joseph in the bible and never let my guard down"
  • 1972 - Went back to commercial and otained a Royal Society of arts certificate RSA
  • 1975 - Got Employed as one of the founding employees of the University of Port Harcourt
  • 1979 - Got married to his beautiful wife
  • 1982 - Got admitted into the Aberdeen College to study for a HND in Secreterial Administration
  • 1984 - Returned to Nigeria for His National Youth Service Corps
  • 1987 - Got a job in the newly formed National Fertilizer Company of Nigeria (NAFCON) where he made several changes and rose to the ranck of Superintendent before the company's privatization in 2008.
  • 1995 - He claims to have received the call of God to open a Missionary ministry known as Chritian Missionary Ministy. The initial operation mode was to train and sponsor young mssionaries into the remote parts of Nigeria and Africa. Along the line the ministry started out a a regulary church
  • 2002 - Became the secretary general of the Eleme branch of the Pentecostal fellowshi of Nigeria (PFN)
  • 2007 -Gained admission into the Redeemer's Bible College for his Master's degree in theology
  • 2017 -Due to Deteriorating Health condition, he relinquished ownership of his church to another ministry called the Charismatic Renewal Ministry
  • 2018 - he dies at the age of 70
